Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a train ticket? +

To book a train ticket, click on the "Book Ticket" link in the navigation menu. Fill in your journey details including source station, destination station, train number, and date of travel. Follow the prompts to complete your booking.

How can I check train schedules? +

Click on the "Train Schedule" link in the navigation menu. Enter the train number you want to check, and the system will display the complete schedule including arrival and departure times at each station.

What are the different classes of travel? +

Indian Railways offers various classes of travel including:

  • 1A (First Class AC) - Most luxurious with private cabins
  • 2A (Second Class AC) - Comfortable berths in an air-conditioned coach
  • 3A (Third Class AC) - Air-conditioned coach with berths
  • SL (Sleeper Class) - Non-AC coach with berths
  • CC (AC Chair Car) - Air-conditioned seating
  • EC (Executive Class) - Premium seating with extra legroom
What is a PNR number? +

PNR (Passenger Name Record) is a unique 10-digit number assigned to your booking. It is used to track your reservation status and is required for any modifications or cancellations.
